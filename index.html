About Us

MISSION

We're a design studio that aims to create wondrous pieces, handmade by talented Egyptian artisans. Our home accessories are handcrafted with creativity and care.

VISION

Our goal is to show the world the creative minds and talented artisans Egypt has. By exporting good quality, creative and artistic products. We can't think of a better way to represent our country internationally!

STORY

Two sisters born and raised in Egypt. Influenced by it’s beautiful art and culture, impressed by all the chaos and charm this country has to offer. we’ve always been mesmerised by the allure of natural materials. Wood, stones, clay and many more. We dreamt about so many exquisite creations that could be done with them!

We strive to create products with designs that leave an impact on its owner from small decorative pieces to a complete home makeover. We're here to help create the vision you have for your home.
