About Us

We Provide Highest Quality Service Since 2013

Two sisters born and raised in Egypt. Influenced by it’s beautiful art and culture, impressed by all the chaos and charm this country has to offer.
As long as we can remember, we’ve always been mesmerised by the allure of natural materials. Wood, stones, clay and many more. We dreamt about so many exquisite creations that could be done with them! With that in mind and our country in heart we were driven to create a brand that represents the beauty of Egypt.

One of our ultimate goals, is to show the world the creative minds and talented artisans Egypt has. By exporting good quality, creative and artistic products. We can’t think of a better way to represent our country.

We support our community!

As a local business, we at Ellie Home Decor realise the importance of community and local organisations that make a difference. From Egyptian artisans to business owners and everyone in between. We reach out and try to influence the lives of men and women, encouraging them to develop their talents and work on their dreams. We totally support and are continuously inspired by their talents, passion and contributions to our community. These people make Egypt a better place to live and work in.We believe that encouraging and supporting one another is the only way to leap forward and succeed together!
