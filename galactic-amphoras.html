Galactic Amphoras

Each and every piece of the galactic amphoras is inspired by the universal beauty and tells a complete different story than the other. The magical part about this collection is its mysterious color integration end result, using the same techniques in glazing but getting a different color and story each time.

Quick Summary

  1. A textured finish, rich palette and beautiful color integrations enliven this striking collection.
  2. Home accessories/Decorative pieces.
  3. Handmade pottery item. Ceramic painted with a multi-tone matte glaze.
  4. Due to the handcrafted nature of this item, expect slight variation in the appearance of each unique piece.
  5. Dishwasher and microwave safe.
  6. Available Colors
  7. Made in Egypt.
